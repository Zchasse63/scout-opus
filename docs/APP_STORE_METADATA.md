## Description

### Short Description (80 chars)
Find and book day passes at gyms worldwide. Your fitness passport for travel.

### Full Description
Scout is the Airbnb for gyms - discover, book, and access fitness facilities anywhere you travel.

**Key Features:**
• **Instant Discovery** - Find gyms near you with real-time availability
• **One-Tap Booking** - Book day passes in seconds with secure payment
• **QR Check-In** - Skip the front desk with digital passes
• **Verified Reviews** - Read authentic reviews from fellow travelers
• **Trip Planning** - Save gyms for upcoming trips
• **Gamification** - Earn badges and climb the leaderboard

**For Travelers:**
Never miss a workout while traveling. Scout connects you with quality gyms in any city, from boutique studios to full-service fitness centers.

**For Gym Owners:**
Reach new customers and fill empty capacity. Scout handles booking, payment, and check-in so you can focus on fitness.
